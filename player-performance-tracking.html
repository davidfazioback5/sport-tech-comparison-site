Platform Summaries

360Player

Best Software-Only Solution
9.5 / 10

360Player is purpose-built for player development. Coaches can log training loads, create long-term development plans, record video clips, and track player readiness — all from a unified dashboard. Unlike Playertek, it doesn't require expensive GPS hardware, making it significantly more accessible for semi-pro and youth academy clubs.

The platform's strength is how it ties player data to individual development journeys, not just aggregate team metrics. This makes it uniquely powerful for academies tracking players over multiple seasons.

Best for: Academies, elite youth clubs, and semi-professional teams wanting software-led performance insight.

Playertek / STATSports

Best GPS Hardware
9.0 / 10

STATSports is the standard in elite GPS tracking hardware. Their Apex pods are used by Premier League clubs, national teams, and top-tier academies globally. The data quality — sprint distances, acceleration, high-intensity zones — is industry-leading. The software platform has improved significantly but remains primarily data-output focused rather than coaching workflow focused.

The main barrier: hardware cost per player can be significant, and you still need additional tools for the broader coaching and development workflow.

Best for: Elite performance environments where raw tracking data quality is the priority.

What to Look for in Performance Tracking Software

The right platform depends heavily on your budget, coaching philosophy, and whether you want hardware-based GPS tracking or a software-only approach. For most clubs below the full-professional level, software-only tools like 360Player deliver more value per pound than hardware-heavy setups. The key questions to ask: Do you need individual long-term development plans or just session-level data? Do your players have the digital literacy to self-report? And what does your coaching staff actually have time to review?
