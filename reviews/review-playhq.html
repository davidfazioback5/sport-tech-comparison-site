What Is PlayHQ?

PlayHQ is the national sports technology platform developed by Sport Australia and delivered in partnership with Deloitte. It is used as the official registration and competition management platform for Football Australia (formerly Football Federation Australia), Cricket Australia, Basketball Australia, and many other Australian national sporting organisations. For clubs affiliated with these bodies, PlayHQ is often the mandated platform — clubs access it through their national or state association. It handles member registration, online payments, competition draws, results, and compliance reporting at a national scale.

Key Features We Tested

National Registration System

PlayHQ powers the official registration flow for millions of Australian sport participants. Clubs affiliated with Football Australia, Cricket Australia, and other bodies register players, coaches, and volunteers through PlayHQ's national database — ensuring compliance with national body requirements and enabling cross-association data sharing.

Competition Management

Fixture scheduling, draws, team sheets, results, and ladders are all managed within PlayHQ. For associations running structured competitions affiliated with a national body, this is the authoritative system of record.

Integrated Payments

Registration fees, insurance levies, and membership payments are processed through PlayHQ's integrated payment system, with funds distributed automatically between clubs, associations, and national bodies according to configured fee structures.

Pricing

PlayHQ is free for clubs to access through their affiliated national or state sporting body. Pricing is negotiated at the national body level. Individual clubs do not pay directly — costs are typically embedded in registration and affiliation fees.
