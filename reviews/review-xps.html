What Is XPS Network?

XPS Network is an Icelandic-developed coaching and athlete management platform used by professional and semi-professional clubs across football, handball, ice hockey, and other team sports. Its roots are in session planning and coaching methodology — giving coaches a structured way to design training sessions, manage drill libraries, and track player development over time.

It has grown to include video analysis, GPS data integration, and communication tools, making it a comprehensive performance coaching hub for clubs with dedicated coaching staff. XPS is used by national federations, professional academies, and elite clubs worldwide.

Key Features We Tested

Session Planning & Drill Libraries

XPS's session planning tools are exceptional. Coaches can build sessions from a rich drill library, annotate with diagrams, and share with assistants or players ahead of training. The ability to build and save reusable practice plans is a standout feature used by professional coaching departments to ensure consistency across age groups.

Video Analysis

Video upload, clipping, and tagging are well-implemented. Coaches can create highlight reels, share individual clips with players, and annotate footage with drawing tools. It's not as deep as dedicated video analysis platforms like Hudl, but for most club coaching workflows it's more than sufficient.

Athlete Development Tracking

Player profiles in XPS track fitness metrics, attendance, assessments, and coaching notes across seasons. This longitudinal view is valuable for academy environments where player progression needs to be documented and handed off between coaches.

GPS & Wearable Integration

XPS integrates with major GPS and wearable providers so load data can be viewed alongside session plans and player profiles. The integration is clean and avoids the need to manage performance data in a separate system.

Pricing

XPS Network is priced on a per-club or per-user basis depending on the plan. Entry-level plans are accessible for semi-professional clubs; full professional packages with unlimited users and all features are quote-based. A free trial is available.

Who Should Use XPS Network?

XPS is the right choice for clubs with active coaching departments that want to systematise session planning and player development. It suits academies, semi-professional clubs, and national programmes. It is not a replacement for club management software — XPS works best alongside a platform like 360Player, Revolutionise, or TidyHQ that handles member admin.
