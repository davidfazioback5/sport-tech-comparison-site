What Is Pitchero?

Pitchero is a UK-based platform that has been serving grassroots sports clubs for over 15 years. Its distinctive offering is combining a club-facing website builder with all the management tools a grassroots club needs — fixture results, league tables, availability, and member comms — all in one subscription.

Pitchero has integrations with The FA (Football Association) in England, making it a popular choice for affiliated football clubs that need to sync fixture and team data.

Key Features

Club Website: Pitchero's website builder is club-specific — auto-populates match results, fixture lists, team news, and player profiles from the management layer. No coding required and it works well for clubs that want a public online presence alongside admin tools.

Pay-to-Watch & Shop: Clubs can offer paid video streams of matches, sell merchandise via an integrated shop, and process membership payments. These are more developed than most comparably-priced tools.

FA Integration: For English football clubs, Pitchero syncs with FA Whole Game System data — reducing double-entry and keeping team/player registrations accurate.

Pricing

Pitchero offers tiered plans from a free basic website up to Pro plans with full management features, payments, and streaming. UK-focused pricing in GBP; plans start from around A$30–60/month for meaningful features.

Who Should Use Pitchero?

UK grassroots clubs — particularly FA-affiliated football clubs — that want a public website alongside management tools and are starting to think about basic commercial features. For richer monetisation, pair Pitchero with our consulting service.
