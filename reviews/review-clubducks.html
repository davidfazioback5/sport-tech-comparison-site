What Is Club Ducks?

Club Ducks is an Australian club management platform built with community sports clubs in mind. It provides the core tools smaller clubs need — online membership, payment collection, basic communications, and event management — in a simple, affordable package. As a newer platform it's still building out its feature set, but it offers strong value for clubs that find more established platforms overly complex or expensive.

Key Features We Tested

Online Membership & Payments

Members can register and pay online through a simple, mobile-friendly flow. Clubs receive payments directly and can configure different membership types and pricing tiers.

Member Communications

Email communications, news posts, and event announcements can be sent to all members or filtered groups. Basic but effective for clubs that primarily need to keep members informed.

Event Management

Club events, training sessions, and social functions can be created and shared with members, with RSVP tracking to help with event planning and catering.

Pricing

Club Ducks is priced affordably for community clubs, with plans starting at a low monthly rate. A free trial is available. Strong value for small to medium clubs.
