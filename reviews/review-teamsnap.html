What Is TeamSnap?

TeamSnap is one of the largest sports management platforms globally, with tens of millions of users primarily across the United States and Canada. It's used across a wide range of sports — from youth soccer and hockey to adult recreation leagues. The platform covers scheduling, rostering, communications, payments, and league management with a level of maturity that comes from over a decade of development and user feedback.

In recent years, TeamSnap has expanded its feature set into registration, store management, and limited commercial features, making it a comprehensive choice for clubs operating in its primary markets.

Key Features

Scheduling & Roster Management: TeamSnap's scheduling tools are among the most refined in the market. League scheduling, conflict detection, and availability management are all handled smoothly. Multi-team structures work well.

Payments & Registration: Payment processing and online registration are deeply integrated. Works best with US banking and payment setups; European clubs may encounter friction.

Communication: In-app messaging, email, and push notifications. Chat threads by team, coaches-only channels, and parent comms are all solid.

Pricing

TeamSnap offers tiered plans by team size. Base plans start at around A$15–18/month per team; organisation-level plans for multi-team clubs are priced at a higher tier. Pricing has increased in recent years.

Who Should Use TeamSnap?

Youth leagues and recreational clubs in North America, and clubs that primarily need scheduling, rostering, and payment management. European clubs should assess whether the US-centric payment infrastructure fits before committing. For performance depth, consider 360Player.
