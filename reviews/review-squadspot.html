What Is Squad Spot?

Squad Spot is a mobile-first team management application designed for grassroots and amateur sports clubs. It focuses on solving the everyday problems of player availability, training and match scheduling, and team communications — replacing the chaos of WhatsApp groups with a structured, purpose-built tool. The platform is deliberately simple and fast to set up, making it particularly accessible for volunteer-run clubs without dedicated admin staff.

Key Features We Tested

Availability Management

Players confirm or decline availability for upcoming training sessions and matches with a single tap. Coaches see a real-time availability summary and can make team selections based on confirmed players — eliminating the back-and-forth of manual availability checking.

Team Communications

Announcements, reminders, and match notifications go directly to players via push notification and email. The feed-style communication keeps everyone informed without requiring coaches to manage separate messaging threads.

Schedule Management

Training sessions and matches can be scheduled and shared with the full squad. Location details, notes, and kit instructions can be attached to each event.

Pricing

Squad Spot offers a free tier for small teams, with paid plans for clubs needing advanced features or multiple teams. Pricing is among the most accessible in the market.
