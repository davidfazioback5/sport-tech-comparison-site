What Is Spond?

Spond is a Norwegian-founded free club management platform that has grown significantly across European grassroots sport. Its core proposition is simple: a clean, easy-to-use tool that lets volunteer club managers handle scheduling, availability, and communication without a subscription fee. The free tier covers the vast majority of what grassroots clubs need. Premium features (payment processing, advanced admin) are available at a modest cost.

For its target audience — amateur clubs, recreational leagues, junior sports — Spond is genuinely excellent. It just isn't designed for clubs that need more.

Key Features

Availability & Events: Spond's standout feature. Members can respond to events with availability, parents can accept on behalf of children, and coaches get instant attendance views. Arguably the cleanest UX of any platform we tested for this specific use case.

Communication: In-app chat, group messaging, and push notifications are all solid. Works well for multi-team clubs with different age groups.

Payments: Basic payment collection (membership fees, event fees) available on the paid tier. Not a full payment management system but functional for most grassroots needs.

Pricing

Core features are free. Spond Club (premium tier with payments and advanced admin) is available at modest per-club pricing. One of the best value propositions in the market for clubs on tight budgets.

Who Should Use Spond?

Grassroots clubs, recreational leagues, junior sports programmes, and any club where volunteer admin burden is the primary challenge. If you need performance tracking, commercial revenue tools, or more sophisticated reporting, consider 360Player.
