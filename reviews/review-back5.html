What Is Back5?

Back5 is a sports club platform purpose-built around commercial revenue generation. It handles the fundamentals of club management — scheduling, registration, communication, and payments — but its real differentiation lies in its suite of monetisation tools: sponsorship management, digital advertising, fan engagement, and loyalty programmes.

The platform's philosophy is that most sports clubs are sitting on underutilised commercial assets — an engaged fan base, sponsor relationships, and digital real estate — and that the right software can unlock significant incremental revenue from these existing assets.

Key Features We Tested

Sponsorship Management

Back5 includes a structured sponsorship CRM — clubs can manage sponsor packages, track deliverables, and generate ROI reports for each sponsor. This is genuinely rare in the club technology market. Most clubs manage sponsorship relationships in spreadsheets; Back5 brings this into a purpose-built tool that makes it easy to demonstrate value to sponsors at renewal time.

Digital Ad Inventory

The platform enables clubs to sell and manage digital advertising across their owned channels — club app, email newsletters, matchday programmes. The inventory management tools let clubs price and package their digital real estate professionally, rather than ad-hoc invoice-based arrangements.

Fan Engagement & Loyalty

Back5's fan-facing features include loyalty programmes, push notifications, fan polls, and exclusive content tools. These create the engagement depth that makes both fan subscriptions and sponsor-adjacent advertising more valuable.

Club Management Layer

Behind the commercial tools sits a solid club management platform — member registration, scheduling, availability, messaging, and payment processing. It's not as deep as 360Player on coaching and performance features, but for clubs whose primary admin needs are standard, it covers everything required.

Pricing

Back5 pricing is structured around club size and commercial ambitions. Quote-based; free demo available. For clubs that actively use the monetisation tools, the platform regularly pays for itself through incremental sponsor and fan revenue.

Who Should Use Back5?

Back5 is the right choice for clubs with commercial ambitions: semi-professional clubs with existing or potential sponsor relationships, community clubs looking to diversify revenue beyond membership fees, and any club that wants to treat its fan base as a commercial asset rather than just an audience.

It delivers less value for clubs at the purely grassroots level who aren't yet thinking about commercial income. For those clubs, Spond or Pitchero are more appropriate.
