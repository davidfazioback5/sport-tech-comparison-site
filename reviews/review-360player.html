What Is 360Player?

360Player is a player development and club management platform designed for the serious end of the sports club market — academies, semi-professional clubs, and elite youth programmes. It brings together tools that coaches and club managers typically cobble together from separate systems: player profiles, training loads, video clips, availability, scheduling, and communication.

The platform is popular across football (soccer), ice hockey, and other team sports in Europe and North America. Its design philosophy centres on the player development journey — creating longitudinal records that follow a player through multiple seasons and coaching transitions.

Key Features We Tested

Player Development Profiles

This is where 360Player genuinely excels. Coaches can build structured development plans for individual players, attach video clips to specific competencies, log training attendance, and track progress against benchmarks over time. The ability to create long-term plans spanning multiple seasons — and hand those records off between coaches — is unique in this market.

Club Management Suite

Beyond performance, 360Player covers scheduling, availability, member management, messaging, and fee processing comprehensively. For clubs managing multiple teams or age groups, the multi-team structure is clean and well-thought-out. The parent/guardian portal is also notably well-implemented for junior programmes.

Video Analysis

Video upload, tagging, and sharing between coaches and players is built in — not bolted on. Coaches can clip specific moments and attach them to player development notes or team analysis sessions. The quality doesn't match dedicated video analysis platforms, but for integrated use it's genuinely useful.

Integrations

360Player offers API access and integrates with popular GPS providers (including STATSports/Playertek), video tools, and HR/management systems. For clubs with existing tech stacks, this flexibility is valuable.

Pricing

360Player doesn't publish pricing publicly — it's quote-based and scales with club size, number of users, and required features. Based on our market research, clubs should expect a mid-range SaaS price point that reflects the platform's pro focus. Free trials are available on request.

Who Should Use 360Player?

360Player is the right choice for: academies tracking player progress across multiple seasons, semi-professional clubs that need coaching data integrated with admin, and any club with a dedicated performance or coaching staff who will actively use the data.
