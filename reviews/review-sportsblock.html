What Is Sports Block?

Sports Block is a scheduling and facility management platform built for sports clubs, associations, and leisure centres that manage courts, fields, and multi-use facilities. It streamlines the booking and allocation of sports venues — handling recurring bookings, one-off events, external hire, and conflict resolution. For clubs that spend significant admin time managing facility access across multiple teams, coaches, and external hirers, Sports Block addresses a genuine pain point.

Key Features We Tested

Venue & Court Scheduling

Create and manage recurring and one-off bookings across multiple courts, fields, or rooms. Conflict detection prevents double-bookings, and the visual calendar makes it easy to see facility utilisation at a glance.

External Hire Management

Clubs can manage external hiring of their facilities — handling bookings, invoicing, and payments from third-party hirers alongside internal team bookings.

Team & Event Coordination

Training sessions, matches, and events can be scheduled and communicated to participants through the platform, reducing the back-and-forth of manual coordination.

Pricing

Sports Block is priced per facility or per club, with options for small single-venue clubs through to multi-site organisations. Contact Sports Block for current pricing.
