What Is Revolutionise Sport?

Revolutionise Sport is a Sydney-based sports management platform that has grown to become the dominant choice for Australian and New Zealand sporting organisations. It serves national governing bodies, state associations, regional leagues, and individual clubs — often as the end-to-end platform managing everything from member registration at the club level through to national competition draws.

Its deep integration across the sporting hierarchy — club, association, governing body — is a standout feature. Data flows up and down the chain without manual re-entry, making it particularly powerful for sports with federated structures like football, netball, cricket, and rugby.

Key Features We Tested

Member Registration & Management

Revolutionise's registration workflows are best-in-class for the ANZ market. The platform handles online registration, insurance collection, working-with-children checks, and payment processing in a single flow. The member database is comprehensive, with configurable fields, document storage, and bulk communication tools.

Competition Management

Fixture generation, ladder management, results entry, and finals calculations are all handled within the platform. For associations running competitions across multiple grades and age groups, this alone makes Revolutionise worth serious consideration.

Financial Management

Fee collection, payment plans, refund processing, and financial reporting are well-developed. The platform integrates with Australian payment gateways and handles GST calculations correctly — a detail that matters enormously for associations dealing with Australian tax obligations.

Pricing

Revolutionise Sport pricing is based on membership volume and module selection. Individual clubs typically access the platform through their association or governing body. Standalone club plans are available and competitively priced for the ANZ market.

Who Should Use Revolutionise Sport?

Revolutionise is the default choice for Australian and New Zealand sports organisations that need a proven, locally-supported platform. It's particularly strong for sports with association structures, competition management needs, and local compliance requirements. Clubs outside ANZ should look at 360Player, TeamSnap, or Pitchero depending on their needs.
