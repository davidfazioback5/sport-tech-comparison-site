What Is Clubworx?

Clubworx is an Australian-founded club management platform originally built for martial arts schools and fitness studios, now used across a wide range of sports clubs and community organisations. It provides a comprehensive suite for managing memberships, automated recurring billing, class and session bookings, attendance tracking, and online waivers — all within a single dashboard. Its billing and payment automation is one of the strongest in the market for clubs with recurring membership models.

Key Features We Tested

Membership & Billing Automation

Clubworx's membership management is its standout feature. Clubs can configure recurring billing plans, set up automated payment retries, and manage direct debit agreements — significantly reducing the administrative overhead of chasing unpaid memberships.

Class & Session Booking

Members book into sessions via a mobile app or web portal. Coaches see attendance rosters, manage capacity, and track no-shows — useful for clubs running structured training timetables.

Attendance Tracking

Check-in via QR code, PIN, or app. Attendance data is stored per member, giving clubs a complete record of participation over time.

Pricing

Clubworx is priced on a per-member or plan basis, with options for small clubs up to large multi-site operations. A free trial is available. Pricing is competitive for clubs with recurring membership income.
