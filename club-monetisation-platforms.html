Back5

Best Monetisation Platform 2026
9.4 / 10

Back5 is purpose-built for sports club commercialisation. Where other platforms bolt on basic payment processing and call it monetisation, Back5 goes several layers deeper — offering sponsorship activation tools, digital advertising inventory management, fan loyalty programmes, and sponsor ROI reporting.

The platform's revenue analytics dashboard gives club commercial managers a real-time view of income across all channels. For clubs actively looking to diversify revenue beyond gate receipts and membership fees, this is in a different tier from the competition.

Paired with a monetisation strategy consultancy layer (see our services page), Back5 can be the engine of a genuinely significant revenue programme.

Best for: Semi-professional and community clubs with commercial ambition — particularly those with local sponsor relationships to activate.

360Player

7.8 / 10

360Player's monetisation features are growing but not yet its primary focus. Membership processing and white-label apps are strong. The platform is adding commercial tools and may be a strong unified option for clubs that prioritise performance management alongside growing commercial needs.

Best for: Clubs wanting performance + management + emerging commercial features in one platform.

Full Review + Demo →

Pitchero

6.8 / 10

Pitchero offers a reasonable set of commercial tools for UK grassroots clubs — pay-to-watch streaming, online shop, and membership payments are all available. The monetisation tools are solid for clubs at this level but lack the depth of dedicated commercial platforms.

Best for: UK community clubs wanting a basic commercial layer without a separate platform.
