Our Commitment to Transparency

ClubTech Compare is an independent review and comparison resource. We are committed to providing honest, unbiased assessments of sports club technology platforms.

Do We Earn Commission?

Currently, ClubTech Compare does not earn referral fees or commissions from any platform featured on this site. Our reviews and recommendations are entirely independent and are not influenced by any commercial relationship.

We connect clubs and associations with platforms that we believe are a genuine fit for their needs. Our goal is to help sports organisations make better technology decisions — not to earn money from those decisions.

Future Commercial Relationships

If we ever enter into a commercial relationship with a platform provider (such as an affiliate arrangement or sponsored content), we will clearly disclose this on the relevant page. Any such arrangement will not affect our editorial scoring methodology, which is applied consistently across all platforms regardless of commercial status.
