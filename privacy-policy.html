Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

ClubTech Compare is an independent sports technology review and comparison resource operated by Back5 Pty Ltd (ABN available on request), based in Australia. Our website is clubtechcompare.com.au. You can contact us at dave@back5.com.au.

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it — for example, when you:

  • Complete our platform finder quiz and submit a contact request
  • Request our free Club Tech Stack Checklist
  • Contact us directly by email

The information we may collect includes your name, club name, email address, and phone number. We do not collect payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and connect you with relevant platform providers
  • Send you the resource you requested (e.g. the checklist PDF)
  • Improve our website and content

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. If you request an introduction to a platform provider, we will share only the information necessary to facilitate that introduction — and only with your consent.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. This data is aggregated and anonymous. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing dave@back5.com.au.

Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (and applicable state/territory laws), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dave@back5.com.au.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at dave@back5.com.au.
